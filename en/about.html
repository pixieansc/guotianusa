Our Mission

For many years, Guotian's mission has been to spread love into the world and has been actively involved in many public welfare initiatives. The company has donated over half a million dollars to various causes including poverty alleviation, disaster relief, and to education programs in rural areas. Guotian is committed to contributing to the harmonious development of society.

Our goal has always been to make the world a better place with warmth and love

- Chairwoman Yanying Huang
