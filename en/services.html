Welcome to Guotian USA

The Guotian group was established in Nanning, China in 2003 and has made significant process and growth in the last two decades. The USA sector was established in March of 2014. Starting with real estate, the company has now expanded into various sectors, including residential communities, hospitality, education, media live streaming, agriculture, and emergency rescue technology.

Autumn Moon Blue Bay

The Blue Bay is a neighborhood located in Nanning developed by Guotian covering 45 acres. There is 25 acres of apartments with 860 homes in total. The residential area is next to a 50 meter commercial area providing excellent amenities and a model kindergarten. The homes currently on sale.

Autumn Moon Lake Kindergarten

Established in 2010, the kindergarten covers an area of 1.6 acres with a beautiful outdoor activity area. The kindergarten benefits from ample sunlight and spacious environment. With a layout that was backed by science, it is a children's paradise that seamlessly integrates greenery, playful colors, and education.

Guotian Agricultural Technology Company

Our agriculture company has always embraced the core philosophy of serving farmers and revitalizing rural communities. The company is dedicated to becoming a leader in modern agricultural development, offering a variety of services: vegetable and fruit cultivation, aquaculture, and food import and export. By utilizing e-commerce, Guotian breaks down geographical barriers, shipping high-quality produce worldwide.

Jingman Hotel

Established in 2020, this stylish hotel was designed and built to meet 5-star standards, featuring a total 80 guest rooms. The hotel is fully equipped with modern amenities and offers a simple yet elegant decor. Located in Nanning, the hotel is at a prime location for tourism with convenient transportation services.

Guanxi Land and Ocean Cultural Media Company

Land and Ocean Cultural Media Company, established in 2021, specializes in various media production services including live streaming, filming, video editing, social media management and promotion, and short video production. The content mainly focuses on traditional dances and cultural performances.

Guanxi Guorui Rescue Technology Company

Established in 2023, our Rescue Technology Company focuses on emergency rescue training, the sale of rescue equipment, and related programs such as emergency training courses and educational tours. Our company aims to enhance preparedness in the face of emergency and provide valuable training and resources to the community.
